Mushroom Radio

Mushroom Radio is a student run radio station from the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague. Initiated with the intention of creating a platform for expression and communication from within the student community.


The radio station, initiated by KABK alumni Jack Bardwell, Ben Earl and Esther Vane was set up as a research tool that eventually developed into a platform for students to use and discuss topics as they will.

KABK Graduation Show 2020

Mushroom Radio will be hosting a special 4-day broadcast to cover the KABK's graduation show this year. We will be putting together a programme that will explore the show through interviews, games, lectures, performances and more!

To create a digital guide to the graduation show we will be running Mushroom Radio together with the following students: Milu Chen & Myra van der Veen, Pum van de Koppel & Kelvin Dijk, Lena Longefay & Omid Kheirabadi, Hannah Mulqueen & Leo Scarin, Guenn Gustina and Farzaneh Noori.
