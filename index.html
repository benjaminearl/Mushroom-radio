Mushroom Radio

Mushroom Radio is a student run radio station from the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague. Initiated with the intention of creating a platform for expression and communication from within the student community.


The radio station, initiated by KABK alumni Jack Bardwell, Ben Earl and Esther Vane was set up as a research tool that eventually developed into a platform for students to use and discuss topics as they will.

Penelope Umbrico

After high demand our guest this week is Penelope Umbrico. Her installations, video, and digital media works utilize photo-sharing and consumer-to-consumer websites as an expansive archive to explore the production and consumption of images.

Listen in as we discuss ideas around collections, collaboration, ownership and the translation of work in the publishing and exhibiting process.

Group Therapy

Group Therapy is the inter-masters project support group for graduating master’s students of @royalacademyofart.thehauge. We meet once a fortnight to share reference, experience and ideas on each other's projects. In doing so we form an alternative space for learning from each other within the institution.
