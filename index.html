Mushroom Radio

Mushroom Radio is a student run radio station from the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague. Initiated with the intention of creating a platform for expression and communication from within the student community.


The radio station, initiated by KABK alumni Jack Bardwell, Ben Earl and Esther Vane was set up as a research tool that eventually developed into a platform for students to use and discuss topics as they will.

Mushroom Pie

We will be broadcasting a one day show by A Finger in Every Pie. A Finger in Every Pie is the exhibition by KABK Fine Arts graduating students of 2020/2021. The exhibition will feature works by 30 students from the Autonoom, Painting/Printmaking and Sculpture department, curated by The Balcony (Arthur Cordier and Valentino Russo). During the Mushroom Pie radio show we will take you on a tasty and tactile tour for the ears and turn the physical exhibition into one that fits the air. There will be readings, interviews, chats, whispers and sounds from the participating artists of the exhibition

KABK Graduation Show 2020

Mushroom Radio will be hosting a special broadcast celebrating this edition of the Dutch Design Week. We will interview KABK students who participated, talk with Denis Oudendijk from Refunc about fundamental design and listen to a DJ set from Frutto and to a mysterious story from Paula Hernandez.
