All Ears for Jina

open call

for Open Day Radio Show
26th November

We are open to all kinds of pre-recorded works (words, podcasts, mixes, soundscapes, and so on and so forth), and proposals for live sessions as well!

Guidelines for submitting:
–Maximum length for the pre-recorded material: up to 40 minutes
–Create a folder named 'Name_Surname_TypeOfContent_Title' which contains the file
–Send to: contact.mushroomradio@gmail.com
(use WeTransfer for big files!)

For live session ideas, send us an email with your idea/plan, and let's talk about it!

DEADLINE: TUESDAY 22ND NOVEMBER

We look forward to hearing your sounds!

Mushroom Radio is a student-run radio, already shaped by many and passed on like a dear treasure. Mushroom Radio pops up in the digital and physical space in order to give students (and more) a platform to share their work, interests, discoveries, music, and so on and so forth.

The intention is to expand and find common, humid ground within departments and disciplines. As its homonymous beings, Mushroom Radio aims to preserve soil biodiversity, and it is kept alive through endless auditive, imaginative, organizational, and collaborative actions and contributions.
