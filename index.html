Mushroom Radio is a student run radio station from the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague. Initiated with the intention of creating a platform for expression and communication from within the student community.

We are excited to announce that Mushroom Radio is going live again from here every Friday afternoon from 14:00pmCET. With the KABK now closed and the only interaction with the school seeming to be through Microsoft Teams, Mushroom Radio is needed now more than ever as a platform of expression and communication from within the student community. We are looking forward to hearing your mixes, readings, spoken word, discussions. Please record them at home using what ever means you have and upload them as a mp3 file. We are accepting any length of piece but please try and keep below 2 hours. Please name them with your name and title of the show, example: “Name Name_Title Title.mp3”.

Upload files here: Radio Uploads

Can’t wait to hear you soon!
