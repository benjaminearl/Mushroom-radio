Expelling Bodies: Rave - a moment to release

On Wednesday we come together to expel our bodies, release our spirits through dance and spontaneous expression. It is a moment to be together, to unload and to recharge to keep going stronger again the next day. A group of staff & students have organised the following programme, everyone is welcome to take part and we will be continuously adjusting the space to the needs called upon by the moment. Please feel free to join in whenever for dance and healing, come together. There will be an open mic for your songs, poems, screams or statements, the stage is yours. Open to all students and staff as well as outside guests!
