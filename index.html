Mushroom Radio

Mushroom Radio is a student run radio station from the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague. Initiated with the intention of creating a platform for expression and communication from within the student community.


The radio station, initiated by KABK alumni Jack Bardwell, Ben Earl and Esther Vane was set up as a research tool that eventually developed into a platform for students to use and discuss topics as they will.

Group Therapy

Group Therapy is back! Once a month we will be inviting a guest to the group and broadcasting our discussions live on Mushroom Radio so that everybody can have a seat at the table.


Group Therapy is the inter-masters project support group by graduating master's students of the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague (KABK). We meet once a fortnight to share research, experience and ideas. In the process we aim to create an alternative space for peer-to-peer learning within the institution.
